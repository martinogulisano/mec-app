Slip (relative speed above threshold)
\[ v_{\mathrm{th}} = 0.001\,\mathrm{m/s},\qquad v_{\mathrm{rel}} = \dot{x} - v \] \[ \dot{x} - v > v_{\mathrm{th}} \;\Rightarrow\; m\ddot{x} + kx = -f_d N \] \[ \dot{x} - v < -v_{\mathrm{th}} \;\Rightarrow\; m\ddot{x} + kx = +f_d N \]
Stick
\[ |\dot{x} - v| \le v_{\mathrm{th}} \;\wedge\; |x| \le \frac{f_s N}{k} \quad\Rightarrow\quad \dot{x} = v,\quad \ddot{x} = 0 \]
Coefficients
\[ f_s = (1+\varepsilon)\, f_d \]
