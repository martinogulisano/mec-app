Quick guide

  • Sliders — adjust system parameters and initial conditions
  • Play / Stop / Reset — control the simulation
  • Scroll to zoom, drag to pan on any plot
  • Double-click a plot to reset the view
  • Scroll the right panel to explore all widgets
  • Day mode — light theme for panels, main view, and plots
Designed for laptop/desktop — not tested on touch devices.
